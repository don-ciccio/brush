# Meadow example — asset credits

The grass and flower meshes in `assets/models/` (Grass1, Grass4, Grass_5,
RedPoppy) are from the **Quaternius Nature Kit**, released under **CC0** (public
domain) — https://quaternius.com/. No attribution is required; it is provided
here as a courtesy.

They are included to demonstrate brush's instanced-foliage system with real
models: a multi-model palette mixed per instance, each variant at its own scale
(see `assets/meadow.def`).
